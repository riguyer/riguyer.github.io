Single cell analysis tools

My favorite tools and software packages for analysis of single cell data

  • Seurat - outstanding and easy-to-use R package for single cell genomics
  • Signac - single-cell chromatin analysis, seamless integration with Seurat
  • Velocyto - Python-based command line tool for calculating spliced and unspliced RNA ratios in single cells
  • Scanpy - Python package for single cell genomics analysis
  • scVelo - Scanpy extension for analysis and visualization of RNA velocity measurements in single cells

Wine

Learn more about wine and wine regions

  • CellarTracker.com - useful site for logging and tracking your collection, also has a massive database of wines with professional and amateur reviews
  • Wine Spectator vintage charts - refernces for the quality of vintages, categorized by region and varietal
  • Winc wine club - Winc is a wine club for beginners, my wife and I learned a lot about varietals and regions by joining several years ago
  • Wine Folly - great website for information on varietals and regions, much of the content is free

Some of my favorite wineries

  • Beekeeper Cellars - small batch Zinfandels, very well made by Ian Blackburn, many remind me of high-quality Rhone Valley GSM blends.
  • Bella Winery - delicious small-batch Zinfandels.
  • Kosta Browne - my wife's favorite winery, both the Pinot and the Chardonnay are excellent. They source grapes from regions throughout California, and have recently started collaborating with growers in Burgundy, too.
  • McKenzie-Mueller Vineyards and Winery - Really nice single-varietal wines, their Malbecs are particularly outstanding. Excellent value.
  • OVID Napa Valley - the recession must be hurting wine sales, because I got off the waiting list about 5 years sooner than expected!
  • RdV Vineyards - Daniel Boloud calls it the best wine west of Bordeaux.
  • Trefethen Family Vineyards - highly recommend the Dragon's Tooth, a Bordeaux-style red blend. The 2017 vintage is particularly good.
